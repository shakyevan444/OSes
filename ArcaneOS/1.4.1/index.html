Attention ArcaneOS Users!

We are thrilled to announce the official release of NexaOS, the next generation of our operating system! NexaOS brings a host of new features, enhanced performance, and a refined user experience.

To upgrade to NexaOS and enjoy all the benefits it offers, click the "Update" button below. This will initiate the update process, ensuring a smooth transition from ArcaneOS to NexaOS.

We have carefully tested NexaOS to deliver a seamless and optimized user experience. This release incorporates valuable feedback and suggestions from our beta testing program, ensuring a stable and reliable operating system for all users.

If you choose not to upgrade at this time, you can continue using ArcaneOS. However, please note that ArcaneOS will no longer receive major feature updates, although we will continue to provide necessary security patches and bug fixes.

Thank you for your support and trust in our operating system. We are confident that NexaOS will exceed your expectations and provide an enhanced computing experience.

Upgrade now and discover the power of NexaOS!
